We are always looking for talented individuals to join our team.

Web Developer

We are looking for a skilled Web Developer who will be responsible for developing and/or designing websites for our company. You will be working alongside a team of other developers in creating, maintaining, and updating our websites.
  • Responsibilities: Write or review code for various applications, run functionality testing and debug code, develop and implement front-end architecture to support user interface concepts.
  • Requirements: Knowledge of some programming languages ( HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc.) and experience with responsive and adaptive design.

Application Developer

We are seeking a talented Application Developer to design and implement new system applications and features.
  • Responsibilities: Creating, maintaining and implementing the source code that makes up the application or program, designing the prototype application, indicate program unit structure, and coordinate application plans with the development team or client.
  • Requirements: Proven experience as an application developer, proficiency in Java or Kotlin, HTML, CSS and JavaScript, strong problem solving skills.

Marketing & Analytics

We are looking for a Marketing & Analytics specialist who can help us in our overall marketing efforts. You will be an integral part of the development and execution of marketing plans to reach targets from brand awareness to product promotion.
  • Responsibilities: Conduct market research to find answers about consumer requirements, habits and trends, brainstorm and develop ideas for creative marketing campaigns.
  • Requirements: Proven experience as a marketing specialist or similar role, thorough understanding of marketing elements and market research methods.

UI/UX Graphic Designer

We are looking for a creative UI/UX Graphic Designer with up-to-date knowledge to interpret our clients needs and to design solutions with high visual impact.
  • Responsibilities: Work on a variety of products, including websites, magazines, exhibitions, corporate identity etc. and you will cooperate with our designers and sales team..
  • Requirements: Proven graphic designing experience, a strong portfolio, ability to interact, communicate and present ideas, up to date with industry-leading software and technologies (In Design, Illustrator,Figma, Photoshop, etc.)
